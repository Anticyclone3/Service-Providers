Frequently Asked Questions

Registration is free. We operate on a commission-based model, taking a small percentage of each completed job booked through our platform. There are no monthly fees or upfront costs to join.

Our verification process includes identity verification, professional credential validation, and background checks. Once you submit your registration, our team will review your information and guide you through the verification steps. The process typically takes 3-5 business days.

Clients pay through our secure payment system. Once a service is completed and confirmed by the client, the payment is processed and transferred to your linked bank account or payment method. Funds are typically available within 2-3 business days after service completion.

Yes, you have complete control over your availability, service area, and pricing. You can update your schedule at any time through your provider dashboard, and you're free to set competitive rates based on your experience and the local market.

We have a dedicated dispute resolution team to handle any issues that may arise between providers and clients. Both parties can submit their concerns through our platform, and our team will work to find a fair resolution based on our service policies and terms of agreement.
