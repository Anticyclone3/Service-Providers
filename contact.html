Frequently Asked Questions

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, we recommend calling our customer support line for immediate assistance.

For account-related issues, please select "Customer Support" in the subject dropdown of our contact form. Be sure to include your account email address and a detailed description of the problem you're experiencing. Our support team will assist you as quickly as possible.

If you've had an issue with a service provider, please select "Customer Support" in the subject dropdown and provide details about your experience. Include the provider's name, the date of service, and a description of the issue. Our customer service team will investigate and work toward a resolution.

For partnership inquiries, please select "Partnership Opportunities" in the subject dropdown. Provide information about your organization and the type of partnership you're interested in. Our business development team will review your inquiry and get back to you to discuss potential collaboration opportunities.
